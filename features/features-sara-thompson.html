Features List:

Description:

As a teacher, I want a simple web-based budgeting tool that requires no account setup for my students As a teacher, I want students to be able to access the app via a link, so they can start working on their budgets As a teacher, I want the tool to have a clean and minimal interface, so students can easily navigate it As a teacher, I need my students to save their work temporarily during the session, so they don’t lose any progress if something happens to their computer As a teacher, I want my students to export their completed budget as a PDF, they can easily print or submit it for their assignment As a teacher, I need my students to be able to input their income and expenses easily, so they can quickly create a budget

As a teacher, I want my students to review and adjust their budget before finalizing it, so I can ensure it’s accurate and balanced

As a teacher, I need my students to see clear labels and indicators relevant to budgeting, so they can better understand the process as they work.

As a teacher, I want the tool to provide preset categories for common expenses, so students can quickly fill out a budget without spending time on unnecessary things

Constraints:

It may be worth having a free or light version that does not require login (the minimum viable product), and a more fully-featured paid version with user accounts.

Comments:

Based on Sara Thompson's Scenarios, using the simplest version of the potential product.
