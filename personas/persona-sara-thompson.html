Persona: Sara Thompson

Sara Thompson, 35, is a middle school teacher, who is somewhat comfortable with tech and various software applications. She wants to find a way to introduce her students to budgeting and personal finance, giving them hands-on experience. She wants a tool that is accessible and easy to understand, use, and navigate without unnecessary complexity. She does not want to use existing tools, like quickbooks, that require account registration or other steps that could be a barrier to students.
