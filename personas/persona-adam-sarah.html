Persona: Adam and Sarah

Adam, age 32, and Sarah, age 30, are a newly engaged couple who are planning a wedding. Adam is a sales representative for solar panels, and he works part-time as a freelance writer. Sarah is an administrative assistant for her local city water district. They do not have any children, but plan to start a family after they get married. Sarah uses software products in her career to keep track of bonds and measure the progress of long-term projects. Adam uses software products to meet with clients virtually but is reluctant as he prefers talking to people in person.
