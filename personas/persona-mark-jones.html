Persona: Mark Jones

Mark Jones, 20, is a college student. He is very familiar with technology, using it everyday in some capacity. Between juggling classes, part-time work, and social activities, he is determined to manage his finances better to ensure he can cover his tuition, rent, and daily expenses without stress. Mark is looking for a budgeting tool that is easy to use, helps him track his spending, and provides insights on how to save money. Mark is tech-savvy and appreciates apps with a clean, intuitive interface. He hopes that by using a budgeting tool, he can build good financial habits that will benefit him long after graduation.
