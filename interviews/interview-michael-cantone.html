User Interview

Interview by: Michael Cantone

Date: 1/24/25

Have you used a budget tracking application before?

Not an application just meant for budgeting. I have used excel spreadsheets to help me organize my spending.

How often do you usually budget and what are you budgeting for?

I get paid every two weeks, so I usually budget on or around my payday. I budget for things like my bi-weekly grocery spending, gas, and rent.

What do you feel budgeting gives you and what do you want to make easier when you budget?

I think budgeting gives me a sense of control and clarity when it comes to my money. I can easily over spend if I am not aware of how much I should be spending on certain things. I would like to be able to have a total amount of money lef tfor the month constantly updated as I update my spending throughout the 2 weeks that I am tracking.

What do you not like about budgeting that you wish you didnt have to do?

The hardest part for me about budgeting is estimating how much I should be spending in my normal expenses. It would be really convenient if a program could suggest amounts to spend in each area so I can just adjust those numbers and not try to figure it out all by my self.
