User Interview

Interview by: Jacob Vallery

Date: 01/22/2025

How do you currently manage your budget? What tools or methods are you using now?

Notebook, paper, envelopes, banking app.

What difficulties do you face with your current budgeting method?

Knowing when bills will be due, knowing how much I can spend on a given day, and how long my current budget will take to open up a bit.

Are there financial goals you have that you feel a budgeting tool will NOT help with?

I’ve been interested in the stock market, and I’m unsure a budgeting tool could help with that.

Do you prefer mobile app or website tools?

Generally mobile apps, but for a budget tool I think I would prefer a desktop situation.

Do you like notification/reminders to remain interactive with the tool? What kind of notifications/reminders do you like most?

I typically turn off notifications if I have the option, being someone with ADHD, they often have the opposite effect on me.

Is customization important to you?

Yes, especially since anything could happen that might change the budget.
