User Stories for: Adam and Sarah

User Stories for scenario #1:

  • As a fiancé, I want to be able to track my spending so that I can save money for my wedding.
  • As a fiancé, I want to calculate changes in my spending and see how it effects my overall budget.
  • As a fiancé, I want to be able to track wedding expenses.
  • As a fiancé, I need a way to organize expenses for the wedding.
  • As a fiancé, I need a way to make sure I do not overspend on the wedding.
  • As a fiancé, I need to be able to see what expenses I might need to allocate to my family.
  • As a fiancé, I need to identify any excess spending.
  • As a fiancé, I need a way to organize my daily living expenses.

User Stories for scenario #2:

  • As a sales representative, I need to be able to track the sales I have made.
  • As a sales representative, I need a way to organize my expenses due to inconsistent pay.
  • As a sales representative, I want to track my spending, so I do not overspend on future expenses.
  • As a sales representative, I want to plan out my expenses, so I get the most out of my paycheck.
  • As a sales representative, I need a way to track my commission.
  • As a sales representative, I need a way to budget my expenses for a set time period.
  • As a sales representative, I want to save enough of my paycheck to bring over to next month’s expenses.
  • As a sales representative, I need help knowing how much of my paycheck should be going to certain expenses.

User Stories for scenario #3:

  • As a medical patient, I need help budgeting my pay so I can afford my medical bills
  • As a medical patient, I need a way to adjust my budget to best fit my goals
  • As a medical patient, I need a way to stretch my money so I can cover my expenses while I’m out of work.
  • As a medical patient, I need to account for new expenses like physical therapy.
  • As a medical patient, I need help understanding how much of my money should go to my daily living expenses.
  • As a medical patient, I need a way to pay my bills and still save money.
  • As a medical patient, I want to afford my daily expenses and what I need for my health.
  • As a medical patient, I need a way to organize my personal expenses and my medical expenses.
